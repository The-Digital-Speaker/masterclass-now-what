As the final frontier of innovation, synthetic biology unlocks longevity, enhances human capabilities, eradicates diseases, and shifts healthcare from reactive to proactive. Unlike classic CRISPR, prime editing can insert, delete, or rewrite DNA with unprecedented accuracy. It's the upgrade from a hammer to a scalpel. AlphaFold 3 from Google DeepMind is a seismic shift in molecular biology — the Rosetta Stone for proteins, decoding life one fold at a time.
