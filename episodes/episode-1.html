Core Concept

The Second Half of the Chessboard

In the ancient tale, Sissa Ben Dahir asked for one grain of rice on the first square, doubling each square. By square 32, the total was 4 billion grains. Manageable. By square 64? More rice than has ever existed on Earth.

We're no longer on the first half. AI compute doubles every six months. Inference costs fell 150× in 18 months. Google's Willow quantum chip solves in 5 minutes what would take the world's best supercomputer 10 septillion years.

The Acceleration Engine

Hyper Moore's Law

Traditional Moore's Law doubled computing power every two years through hardware miniaturization. Hyper Moore's Law — coined by NVIDIA CEO Jensen Huang — doubles or triples AI computing performance annually.

This isn't just about faster chips. It's driven by a convergence of software, networking, algorithms, and hardware infrastructure working in concert.

The result? What once required the resources of global hyperscalers is now within reach for startups. DeepSeek-R1 rivals GPT-4 at just 5% of the operating cost. The "only Big Tech can play" era may be over.

Case Study

From Outback to Intelligence Age

The Challenge

In 2011, Mark and his friend Reinier set out to cycle 14,122 km around Australia in 100 days — just the two of them, no support crew, battling desert heat and impossible odds.

The Lesson

Every pedal stroke felt insignificant against the vastness — yet each one compounded. Discipline, adaptability, and a long-term vision carried them through the Nullarbor's 1,200 km of nothing.

The Parallel

Exponential change works the same way. Early progress feels invisible. Then suddenly, you've crossed a continent — or disrupted an entire industry.

The Result

14,122 km completed. €25,000 raised. A mindset forged: "Thriving isn't about reaching the finish line. It's about how you choose to ride the journey."

The Digital Renaissance

Unlike the European Renaissance (centuries), this revolution happens in years. Technologies converge like Lego blocks — AI with blockchain, spatial intelligence on biotech — generating entirely new industries in record time.

Supercooled Water

Disruption happens like supercooled water — liquid below freezing until one trigger crystallizes everything at once. Gradually, then suddenly.
